Mean Features (Average measurements of the tumor)
mean radius → Average distance from tumor center to boundary points (overall tumor size).
mean texture → Average variation in gray-scale intensity (surface appearance/roughness in image).
mean perimeter → Average boundary length around the tumor.
mean area → Average total area occupied by the tumor.
mean smoothness → How smooth or irregular the tumor boundary is.
mean compactness → How compact/dense the tumor shape is.
mean concavity → Degree of inward curves or indentations in the tumor boundary.
mean concave points → Number of inward-curving points in the tumor boundary.
mean symmetry → How symmetrical the tumor shape is.
mean fractal dimension → Complexity/roughness level of the tumor boundary pattern.
Error Features (Variation or inconsistency in measurements)
radius error → Variation/error in tumor radius measurements.
texture error → Variation/error in texture measurements.
perimeter error → Variation/error in perimeter measurements.
area error → Variation/error in area measurements.
smoothness error → Variation/error in smoothness measurements.
compactness error → Variation/error in compactness measurements.
concavity error → Variation/error in concavity measurements.
concave points error → Variation/error in concave point measurements.
symmetry error → Variation/error in symmetry measurements.
fractal dimension error → Variation/error in fractal dimension measurements.
Worst Features (Most extreme/worst-case measurements)
worst radius → Largest observed tumor radius.
worst texture → Most irregular texture observed in the tumor.
worst perimeter → Largest observed tumor perimeter.
worst area → Largest observed tumor area.
worst smoothness → Highest irregularity in tumor smoothness.
worst compactness → Highest compactness/density observed.
worst concavity → Highest inward-curving severity in tumor boundary.
worst concave points → Maximum number of inward-curving points observed.
worst symmetry → Most asymmetrical tumor shape observed.
worst fractal dimension → Highest complexity/roughness in tumor boundary.
Most Important Intuition

These features are basically trying to mathematically describe:

tumor size
tumor shape
tumor boundary irregularity
tumor complexity

Because:

benign tumors are usually smoother and regular
malignant tumors are often larger, irregular, asymmetric, and complex

So the dataset converts medical images into numerical measurements that ML models can learn from.
